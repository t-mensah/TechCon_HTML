History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in a local community center. It was born from a desire to create a platform for sharing knowledge and fostering innovation outside of large corporate settings. Over the years, TechCon has grown exponentially, attracting thousands of attendees and becoming a key event in the tech industry calendar. The event has moved from small meeting rooms to major convention centers, reflecting its expanding influence and community.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between budding technologists and industry leaders. We strive to create an inclusive and dynamic environment where everyone, from students to seasoned professionals, can learn, connect, and collaborate. Our core principles revolve around promoting accessible technology, fostering sustainable innovation, and building a strong, supportive community.
